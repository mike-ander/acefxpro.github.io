Introducing Business Partner

AceFXPro Introducing Business Partner program has been designed to remunerate those individuals or companies that refer new clients who open live trading accounts with us. Each time one of your referred clients executes a trade your account will be credited with a rebate in real time. There is no limit to the number of clients or trades that will earn this rebate.

Maybe you're just introducing us to a few friends or a trading group you belong to. Perhaps you write your own trading blog or website, or you are an experienced trader who is mentoring other traders, whatever the situation AceFXPro would like to help you along and reward you with an income stream in the process.

As an IB for AceFXPro you can be confident that the products and services you are introducing are among the best in the Forex and CFD market. Because we utilise enhanced technology this allows us excellent speed and accuracy of order execution. Combining this with one of the world's best, most popular trading platforms creates an excellent environment for your friends, colleagues or customers to trade in.

Register as an IB and get,

  • High rebate payout
  • Fast and easy client onboarding
  • Rebate generated into your account in real time
  • Personal portal allows you to keep track of details such as visitors, registrations and clients trades
  • Dedicated account manager
