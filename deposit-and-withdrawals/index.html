Security of Funds

Our client funds are completely segregated from the company funds, and are held in separate

bank accounts. This is in accordance with industry regulations to ensure that all client funds are protected.

AceFXpro adheres to stringent guidelines by the authorities, which includes Anti-Money Laundering policies to protect all clients. We do not accept third party deposits or withdrawal requests.
